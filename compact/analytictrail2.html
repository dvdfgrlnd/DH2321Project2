Analytic trail 1 (Private vs state ownership in selected countries)

Research question

Does the largest countries (based on GDP and sorted by continent) attitude toward private vs state ownership of business correlate with the countries GDP growth?

Start

Selected the countries and dragged the time line in order to get an brief overview by the radar chart.

Find countries min and max

Now the countries min and max GDP growth was found by dragging the time line.

Prepare for comparison

Right clicking a circle creates a copy with the current year to identify it.

Compare

Hovering over the circles displays the overview (radar chart), and details (list). That makes it easy to compare values different years. The text ('>private', '>state', '~') was added afterwards for clarity and shows if private or state ownership increased with GDP growth ('~' means no difference).

Insights

There doesn't seem to be a correlation beteween the populations attitude towards private ownership and GDP growth.
This is based on the fact that only two of the six countries GDP growth occured when private (or state) attitude was at an maximum.
The tool made it easier to get an overview of the answers and utilised different dimensions to display data which lead to a more compact tool.
It could be improved by an automatic way to find minium and maximum values, and multiple selection for automatic comparison.
