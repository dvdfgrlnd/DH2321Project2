This tool is used to enhance the exploration of each continents largest country (in terms of GDP) and the correlation between GDP growth and the populations attitude towards private and state ownership of business.

  • Countries on y-axis
  • GDP growth (percent) on the x-axis
  • Circle size indicate attitude (small circle -> increased private ownership)
  • Black circle indicate no data avaible
  • Click circle to select
  • Right click circle to create a static copy of a circle
  • Click static copy to remove the circle
  • Drag the slider to change year
  • Hover over a circle to show details
